MIDI Guidelines and Rules

By downloading my MIDIs, you agree to follow my guidelines and rules listed below.

I. Public MIDIs

1. Uploading my MIDIs

You may upload my MIDIs to any platform, as long as you give the authors proper credit. Proper credit consists of linking the original video made by me in the description or something similar.

2. Download Links

If you will provide a download link to someone or in a video, you must either give the link to the MIDI's page or the direct download link obtained from the MIDI's page. You are not allowed to create your own download link by uploading the MIDI file to your own website or something similar. All links must be the true original link, for the reason that I may change the download link in the future, or privitize the MIDI.

3. MIDIs that have gone private

In the case that a MIDI has gone private, the download link will be removed from the MIDI's page. There will be no notice given to anyone who has downloaded the MIDI. If you have a MIDI that is now private, you may continue to use it for your own personal use. You will then be required to follow the guidelines and rules for private MIDIs from that point on.

II. Private MIDIs

1. Uploading my MIDIs

Please contact me if you would like to upload my private MIDIs to a platform. Uploading a private MIDI without my permission will result in you being banned from getting my future private MIDIs.

2. Download Links

Download links for private MIDIs are not allowed to be shared. If you would like to get a private MIDI, please contact me. Do not get it from someone else.

3. General Rules of Thumb

If you are unsure if you are allowed to do something regarding a private MIDI, please contact me. I won't bite. I will be more than happy to answer any questions or concerns you may have.

III. Other Rules

1. Modifications

You are allowed to modify my MIDIs, which includes editing the MIDI, remaking the MIDI, or remixing the MIDI. You are not allowed to claim the MIDI as your own. Proper credit must still be given to the authors. You are also not allowed to take away parts of the MIDIs and use them in a way that does not modify the original MIDI. For example, you are allowed to use it my MIDIs in remixes, but you are not allowed to take arts from said MIDI and use it in a different MIDI.

2. Begging

You are allowed to ask for a MIDI, but you are not allowed to beg for a MIDI. If you were rejected for a MIDI, please do not consistently ask for it. This is annoying and will result in you being banned from getting my future private MIDIs.

2. Exceptions

Exceptions exist for all rules. If you have a question or concern, please contact me. Most of the time I will make exceptions for certain things, or for certain people. Generally speaking, if you are a good person, I will be more than happy to make exceptions for you.
